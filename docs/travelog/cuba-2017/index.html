    Leaving Town #

    In the summer of 2017 I had the opportunity to visit Cuba with some co workers. One of them took a lot more photos than I did, which is good because mine weren’t very good. I packed my bag and I was ready to go.

    We packed so much into about a week, and took so many pictures, I’m afraid there’s no way to do it justice. It was incredible, we drank rum, played on the beach, rode horses into the jungle, smoked cigars, enjoyed art, architecture, history, dancing, music, and on and on. I mean you name it. It was one of the most incredible experiences of my life.
