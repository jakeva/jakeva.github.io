    Europe Part 1 - Ireland #

    Dublin #

    I flew into Dublin via JFK. I stayed a few days to catch up with a colleague who lived there and we may or may not have enjoyed copious amounts of the finest Irish beverages.

    Howth #

    I did some sight seeing, and on my last day took the train up to Howth for some really beautiful views of the small port city.

    Ferry #

    Eventually I made my way to a ferry that took my around the southern part of England and straight to Normandy, France. I arrived on my birthday.
